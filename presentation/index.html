Core Capabilities

Privacy Without
Compromise

NixWallet wraps the full power of Fhenix's Fully Homomorphic Encryption into a seamless Chrome extension—giving you encrypted token management, multi-network support, and a suite of privacy tools.

FHE Token Wrapping

Seamlessly wrap any ERC-20 token into a fully homomorphic encrypted variant. Your balances and transactions remain confidential on-chain.

Multi-Account Management

Derive unlimited HD accounts from a single seed phrase, or import external private keys. Switch between wallets instantly.

Multi-Network Support

Currently live on Ethereum Sepolia. Multi-network support for Base testnet and Arbitrum testnet (alongside Sepolia) is coming soon.

Coming Soon

Address Book

In-Wallet Swaps
