NIXWALLET

A confidential browser wallet built on the Fhenix network, powered by Fully Homomorphic Encryption.

The Problem

On-Chain Data Is Public

Most wallets today expose your balance, your transfers, and your activity on a public ledger. Anyone can look it up. There's no built-in confidentiality layer for EVM users.

The Solution

Compute On Encrypted Data

Fhenix uses Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) — smart contracts can process your tokens without exposing the underlying data. NixWallet brings this directly to your browser.
